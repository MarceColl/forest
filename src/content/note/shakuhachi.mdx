The [Shakuhachi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shakuhachi) is a japanese bamboo flute,
traditionally used for Suizen, blowing meditation by the buddhist monks of the Fuke sect,
the [Komuso](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Komus%C5%8D).

<img src={k.src} alt="A komuso monk, with a basket over their head, playing the shakuhachi" />

* [Honkyoku sheet music](https://sound.jp/mujuan/music.html)
* [Several good articles about the shakuhachi](https://flutedojo.com/shakuhachi-guides) by Jon Kypros
