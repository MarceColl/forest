The <EL text="Shakuhachi"
    link="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shakuhachi"
    archive="http://archive.dziban.net:8000/archive/1724232182.55671/singlefile.html" /> is a japanese bamboo flute,
traditionally used for Suizen, blowing meditation by the buddhist monks of the Fuke sect,
the [Komuso](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Komus%C5%8D).

<img src={k.src} alt="A komuso monk, with a basket over their head, playing the shakuhachi" />
